Last updated: September 23, 2025

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to website owners.

How We Use Cookies

IronSnout.fun uses cookies for the following purposes:

1. Advertising Cookies

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our website. Google AdSense uses cookies to:

  • Show relevant advertisements based on your interests
  • Measure ad performance and effectiveness
  • Prevent the same ad from being shown too many times
  • Report on ad impressions and interactions

2. Analytics Cookies

We may use analytics services to understand how visitors use our website. These cookies help us:

  • Count the number of visitors to our website
  • Understand which pages are most popular
  • Improve our website's performance and user experience

Third-Party Cookies

Our website may contain content from third parties, including:

Google AdSense

Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

Game Content

The embedded Iron Snout game may use its own cookies. Please refer to the game provider's privacy policy for more information.

Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in several ways:

Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to:

  • View what cookies are stored on your device
  • Delete cookies individually or all at once
  • Block cookies from specific websites
  • Block all cookies from being set
  • Delete all cookies when you close your browser

Advertising Opt-Out

You can opt out of personalized advertising through:

  • Google Ads Settings
  • Digital Advertising Alliance Opt-Out
  • Your Online Choices (EU)

Impact of Disabling Cookies

Please Note

Disabling cookies may affect your experience on our website. Some features may not work properly, and you may see less relevant advertisements.
